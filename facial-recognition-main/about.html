About Us

Welcome to our innovation hub! At Smart Indian Hackathon 2023, our team, "Debug Thugs," envisioned a future where cutting-edge technology becomes a cornerstone of public safety. Passionate about harnessing advancements in AI and computer vision, we set out to revolutionize crowd management, object detection, and criminal identification. Our journey began with a simple idea: using technology to make the world a safer place. Through dedication and innovation, we have turned that idea into reality.

Our mission is to create solutions that empower communities and enhance public safety measures. We believe in the power of artificial intelligence and real-time data analysis to revolutionize the way we approach security. By seamlessly integrating AI-powered object detection and crowd management systems, we aim to create safer environments for everyone.

Our Vision

Our vision is to bridge the gap between technology and societal security. We strive to create a future where communities are empowered with intelligent solutions. Imagine a world where public spaces are not only safe but also smart. With our innovative criminal detection system, we are adding an extra layer of security to make this vision a reality.

At Debug Thugs, we are not just a team; we are a movement towards a safer world. Join us in our mission to revolutionize public safety and make a lasting impact on society.
